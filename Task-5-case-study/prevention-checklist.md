Social Media Prevention Checklist
 Account Security
Use strong and unique passwords
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
Don’t share your login details with anyone
 Privacy Protection
Keep your profile private
Avoid sharing personal info (address, phone, location)
Check privacy settings regularly
 Safe Usage
Don’t accept requests from unknown people
Avoid clicking suspicious links
Verify news before sharing
 Responsible Behavior
Don’t post offensive or harmful content
Avoid cyberbullying and arguments
Think before you post
 Awareness & Action
Report fake accounts or suspicious activity
Block users who harass or scam
Log out from shared/public devices
